Mall Monsters

Mall Monsters is a game currently in development by Zeros and Ones Game Studio. The game is a escape game where players must navigate a shopping mall filled with quirky monsters, solving puzzles and avoiding obstacles to reach the exit. The game features colorful graphics, engaging gameplay, and a dark story that appeals to players how like and/or can tolerate scariness.

Concept

The concept or idea of the game is like a stor / RPG / addventure horror game where you have to try and survive the mall whiles monsters are chasing you and you have to come up with ways to escape the mall. The game is designed to be challenging and engaging, with a focus on exploration, puzzle-solving, and survival.

Design Notes

  • Play sessions are longer and focused on quick decisions.
  • Characters like to stay in the shadows only coming out to attack.
  • Sound and visual feedback drive player satisfaction for each interaction.
