About

Zeros and Ones Game Studio is a independent game development studio dedicated to creating immersive and engaging gaming experiences. Our team works tirelessly to bring innovative ideas to life. We believe that the power of games helps to entertain, inspire, and connect people across the globe.

Our first game, Mall Monsters, is a thrilling adventure that takes players on a journey through a haunted shopping mall filled with quirky monsters and a plot twisting storyline.

For more information about our studio and our games, please visit our About and Games pages.
